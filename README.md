# Animal Math Safari 🐾

A colourful browser math game for kids to practise the four core
operations - **addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division** -
with a cast of animal friends that pop up to celebrate every correct
answer.

## How to play

1. Open `index.html` in a browser.
2. Pick which operations to practise (+, −, ×, ÷) and a difficulty level.
3. Choose how many questions you want in the round.
4. Type each answer and press **Check** (or hit Enter).
5. Every correct answer summons an animal friend 🐶🐱🐰 - streaks of 3+ get two!

## Difficulty levels

| Level  | Number range | Times/divide cap |
|--------|--------------|------------------|
| Easy   | 0-10         | 10               |
| Medium | 0-20         | 12               |
| Hard   | 1-50         | 15               |

Subtraction problems always have a non-negative answer, and division
problems always divide cleanly into a whole number.

## The animal cast

Dogs, cats, rabbits, foxes, bears, pandas, lions, tigers, koalas, monkeys,
penguins, frogs, pigs, cows, giraffes, elephants, zebras, kangaroos,
turtles, owls, ducks, chickens, horses, unicorns, and butterflies all take
turns cheering you on.

## Files

- `index.html` - game layout and screens (setup, quiz, results)
- `style.css` - safari-themed styling + animal pop-up animation
- `script.js` - problem generator, scoring, streak logic, animal celebrations
